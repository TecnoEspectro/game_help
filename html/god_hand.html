    Short description


    God Hand is a Beat'em up developed by Clover Studios and distributed by Capcom.

    The game tells the history of Gene, the god hand, who figths whit demons around the world, in order to stop their regimen and evil plans.
    The history has impacting moments, memorable moments, fun and comedic moments; is entertaining and enjoyable.

    The game has high playability and is fun, having a score system that appears in the end mission screen, has a moveset that the players make themselves buying or obtaining the moves and equiping them, and the powers is the same history but not in movesets.

    The player has variety of powers and moves, for the powers we have the god hand roulette that shows special powers, and the god hand bar that activates the god hand mode; for the moves we have a high cantity of punches and kicks.
